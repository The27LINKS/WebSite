FAQ

The obvious questions, answered.

Is it really free?
Yes — all 12 tools are free, with no limits on file count or size (other than what your device can comfortably handle). We show a small display ad; that pays the bills.
Where are my files uploaded?
Nowhere. Every tool runs locally using JavaScript APIs (pdf-lib, Canvas, pdf.js). Your files stay on your device — we literally cannot see them.
Why "The27LINKS"?
Because it is the plateform build by developers for real developers. And you are going to love it after using it.
Do the tools work offline?
Once a tool page is loaded, most of them keep working even without internet (they use CDN libraries that get cached by your browser).
Can I request a new tool?
Absolutely — drop us a line on the contact page. Useful, focused utilities always jump the queue.
